Important Information ▼
Pay ▼

All of my work is completely free! I do this for the experience and to improve my skills.

If you'd like to support me, tips are appreciated but never required. (if you do plan to tip I can only accept robux/giftcards)

The only requirement is that you credit me for my work by mentioning me in anyway (@im.bugland) in the game, video, etc. you plan to use my work in.

How to Request a Model ▼

To request a 3D model:

  1. Message me on Discord (@im.bugland)
  2. Include a detailed description or reference images of what you want
  3. Mention your intended use (game, rendering, etc.)
  4. Specify any technical requirements (poly count, format, etc.)

I'll review your request and let you know if I can take it on!

Misc. ▼

Turnaround Time: Since I do this work for free, I can't guarantee specific deadlines. Models may take 1 day or up to 2 weeks depending on complexity. I'll alert you of delays if possible

Rights: You're free to use the models I create for you in your projects, but I will most likely display them in my portfolio and mention you or your project.

Revisions: I offer 1-2 rounds of revisions depending on the complexity of the request.

File Formats: I typically provide .blend, .fbx, and .obj files. Texture files may also be sent separately.
